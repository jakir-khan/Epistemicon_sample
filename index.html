About Epistemicon

Epistemicon, hosted by Andhra Loyola Institute of Engineering and Technology, is a dynamic inter-collegiate fest celebrating innovation, creativity, and talent. It brings together students from diverse colleges to compete, collaborate, and showcase their skills across technology, adaptability & feasibility.
